《Noelle》是一部以「聖二代」為主軸的故事，安娜坎卓克在電影裡扮演聖誕老人的女兒 Noelle ，但比爾哈德飾演的哥哥 Nick 卻百般不願意接管家業，甚至從北極逃去美國，這讓 Noelle 非常傷腦筋，決心將哥哥找回，拯救聖誕節。其他卡司還包括《惡鄰纏身2》(Neighbors 2: Sorority Rising) 比利埃西納 (Billy Eichner) 飾演的家族成員—書呆子 Gabriel ，他會讓聖誕老人的傳統送禮方式有所改革，而影后莎莉麥克琳 (Shirley MacLaine) 也有參與電影，將飾演陪伴 Noelle 找哥哥的小幫手。

    Means well, but doesn't come out with anything memorable. I did enjoy Anna Kendrick in the role of 'Noelle', she keeps the film moving along fairly well. It's the humour and writing which is the issue. The former is hit-and-miss, some of it lands but a lot of it doesn't - the running joke about the Apple product is lame. The latter, meanwhile, is pretty plain and predictable, there's nothing all that fresh about this - except the, bare minimum, positive message. The special effects aren't great either. Fine for an Xmas watch, but nothing more unfortunately.
