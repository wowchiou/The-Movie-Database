    The Hyperions envisages itself as an atypical superhero story, however, it merely proves yet again that being different is not the same as being good. For a superhero movie, there is a noticeable lack of super powers. For a comedy, laughs are few and far between. The script was poorly written, with both a major plot device and a supporting character being seemingly forgotten by the movie's end. The movie's more artistic touches, such as hand drawn animation, were appreciated but ultimately made little impact on the overall outcome. All in all, I found the movie's trailer to be far more enjoyable than the actual film.
